Best Ways To Access Banned Books

written by RichCat06 on July 9, 2022

Demand for books banned in republican states is rising. However, when it comes to accessing books banned in republican states, it's surprisingly easy to access. Without further ado, let's get started.

Library Genesis

Why get a digital library card when you can have access to any ebook you could ever think of for free, with no strings attached? Library Genesis is basically a place where you can download any ebook, again, for free, with no strings attached. There are multiple mirrors you can access their database from, like this one or this one. There is a subreddit if you ever run into a problem or need help. Side note: some of Library Genesis' servers are based in Russia, so expect some slowdowns with the mirrors. You may not need it, but it is recommended that you use a private web browser, like Tor, Brave, or LibreWolf to hide your footprints. Trust me, you don't want a paper trail.

Digital Library Cards In Democratic States

If you don't want to break the law, you can sign up for a digital library card in a state where books aren't banned.
