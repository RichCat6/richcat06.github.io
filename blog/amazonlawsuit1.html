Amazon is selling "suicide kits" to minors, and it pisses me off

written by RichCat06 on October 8, 2022

Amazon has recently gotten into some serious trouble with the law for selling "suicide kits" to minors, and has actually led to a few deaths using a deadly chemical known as Sodium Nitrite.

If you or a loved one are experiencing suicidal thoughts, call the suicide hotline at 1-800-273-8255. Suicide is no joke.
