Internet Explorer To Be Retired Today

written by RichCat06 on June 15, 2022

THE END OF AN ERA

Microsoft is retiring its 27-year-old browser today after it announced a year ago that the browser would be retired today, a move that surprises some. Businesses may experience some issues with the browser as its inevitable retirement happens today. I recommend using something better, like Mozilla's Firefox or Brave. (These are not affiliate links. Just recommendations for a better web browsing experience.)
